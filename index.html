🎯 Our Vision

RAK Domain is a free, browser-based training simulator designed to prepare job seekers for Data Entry Operator (DEO) and Timekeeper positions in Gulf companies — used by companies across the Gulf region and similar organisations.

Our platform replicates the exact SpreadSheet-based workflows used inside Gulf HR and Payroll departments, helping you build real speed, accuracy, and field-specific knowledge before your first day on the job. Practice on authentic employee records, weekly timesheets, OT calculations, and sensitive data handling — all in a safe, offline-style environment.

Whether you are targeting a 25 WPM entry-level role or aiming for Senior DEO / Senior Timekeeper status at 50+ WPM with 98%+ accuracy, RAK Domain tracks your progress, grades your sessions, and shows you exactly where to improve.

🔒 Privacy Policy

Effective immediately. Please read carefully before using this platform.

1. No Real Data Collection
RAK Domain does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personally identifiable information (PII). All session data (WPM scores, accuracy, streaks) is stored only in your browser's local storage and never leaves your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no login system.

2. Simulated Data Only
All employee names, passport numbers, Iqama numbers, bank account numbers, salaries, phone numbers, and timesheet records shown inside this training tool are computer-generated fictional data. They do not represent any real person, company, or account. Any resemblance to real persons is entirely coincidental.

3. STRICT WARNING — Do Not Enter Real Credentials

  • ⛔ Do NOT enter your real passport number, Iqama number, or Emirates ID into any field.
  • ⛔ Do NOT enter real bank account numbers, credit card numbers, or financial data.
  • ⛔ Do NOT enter your real name, mobile number, date of birth, or address.
  • ⛔ Do NOT enter real employee records from your current or previous employer.
  • ⛔ Do NOT enter login credentials, passwords, or access codes of any kind.

The sensitive fields (Passport No., Iqama, Bank Account, Mobile, Time In, Time Out, OT Hours etc.) are intentionally zeroed or masked in the source documents. You must type the masked version shown — not your real data. This is a training simulation. Treat all data as fictional.

4. No Cookies or Tracking
This platform uses no tracking cookies, no analytics scripts, and no third-party services. No data about your usage is sent anywhere.

5. Children's Privacy
This platform is intended for users aged 16 and above preparing for professional employment. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
